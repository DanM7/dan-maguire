Who I Am

I'm an engineer and creator who enjoys solving problems with equal parts structure and imagination. I’ve always worked in spaces where technical systems and creative expression reinforce each other.

What Drives Me

I care about how things work, how they evolve over time, and how structure meets imagination — whether I’m architecting a platform or tinkering with a side project.

How I Think

I approach engineering as a craft: part logic, part storytelling, part design. I believe the best systems are the ones that make complexity feel simple, and the best teams are the ones that grow together through clarity, curiosity, and shared purpose.

Why This Site Exists

This space is a home for the projects, ideas, and experiments that reflect how I think and build. I also use this space to jot down brief reflections on what has worked well in my career: not as guidance for others, but as a way to remember the patterns that matter. It’s a place where engineering discipline and creative exploration meet, and where I get to share the work that doesn’t always fit inside a job title.

Some of these projects start as things I build with my kids. They're simple, curious experiments that keep us connected to the joy of learning and making.
